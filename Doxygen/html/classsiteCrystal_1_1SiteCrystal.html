Detailed Description

Description:

A check is run to verify subscription status. Non-subscribed users will see a short list of search phrases/strings. Whereas a paid up subscriber will be able to customise their search list . Multiple passes are required to highlight each of the search strings. Longest phrases should be searched first, as substrings once highlighted will not allow detection to work.

Site Text: Tools to support your online business Monitor your site for the use of sensitive words and phrases which may compromise your companies ethical integrity or reputation. Automate the detection and verification of copyrighted or trade marked content within your companies web site. Configure periodic checks daily, week;y or monthly to catch issues before they can be used against your business.
